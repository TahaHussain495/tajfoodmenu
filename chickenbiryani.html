Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a beloved and aromatic dish that stands as a culinary masterpiece in South Asian cuisine. It is a flavorful one-pot meal made by layering fragrant basmati rice with spiced and marinated chicken, then slow-cooking them together to perfection.. Loved for its complexity of flavors and satisfying texture, Chicken Biryani is a centerpiece at celebrations, festivals, and everyday meals.
