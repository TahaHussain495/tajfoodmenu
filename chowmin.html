Chowmin

Chow Mein, meaning "stir-fried noodles" in Mandarin, is a beloved dish originating from Chinese cuisine. Over the years, it has become a staple worldwide, with countless regional variations that reflect local tastes and ingredients. The dish is known for its simplicity, versatility, and the satisfying combination of chewy noodles, crisp vegetables, and flavorful sauces.
