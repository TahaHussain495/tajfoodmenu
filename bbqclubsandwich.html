BBQ Club Sandwich

The BBQ Club Sandwich is a hearty and flavorful twist on the classic club sandwich, combining the smoky essence of barbecue with layers of delicious fillings. This triple-decker delight typically features tender slices of grilled or barbecued chicken, crispy bacon, and melted cheese, stacked between toasted bread slices. Slathered with tangy barbecue sauce and complemented by fresh lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and sometimes caramelized onions, this sandwich is a symphony of textures and tastes. Perfect for a filling lunch or dinner, the BBQ Club Sandwich is often served with a side of crispy fries or coleslaw, making it a satisfying treat for barbecue and sandwich lovers alike.
