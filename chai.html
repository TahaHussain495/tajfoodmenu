Chai

Chai is more than just a beverage; it's a cultural ritual and a beloved tradition, particularly in South Asia. A spiced tea made from a blend of black tea leaves, milk, sugar, and an aromatic mix of spices like cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and ginger, chai offers a comforting, warm, and flavorful experience. Chai has become famous worldwide not only for its bold, soothing taste but also for the sense of warmth and hospitality it symbolizes. It is a drink that brings people together, offering a moment of relaxation and connection in the midst of busy lives.
